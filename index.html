Pulse Dance Studio: West Coast Swing in Auckland, New Zealand

Located at TAPAC - The Auckland Performing Arts Centre, 100 Motions Road, Western Springs, 1022

By car: TAPAC is close to the St Lukes motorway offramp and Great North Road. (There is a huge free carpark at the front of the building for your use.)

Pulse Dance Studio offers West Coast Swing group classes, private lessons, workshops, social activities and events in Auckland, New Zealand.

Thursdays - Weekly:
Level 1 (Beginner) Group Class: From 8:30PM
Social Dancing: 9:15-10:30PM
Level 2 Group Class: From 7:30PM *Assessed, eligible students only*

West Coast Swing is fun to watch and fun to do! Our tailored curriculum and expert staff make West Coast Swing easy to learn!

West Coast Swing may be danced (socially and competitively) to a variety of music, including traditional Swing and Blues, Soul, Funk, Disco, Pop, Rock, Hip Hop, contemporary RnB and lyrical acoustic music... The music that you could expect to hear in bars, at parties or on the radio.
